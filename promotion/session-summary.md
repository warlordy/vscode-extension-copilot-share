## Copilot Share: Turn Crowded AI Chats into Clear Outcomes

When using Copilot or other AI agents, sessions can quickly become crowded with mixed topics and too much detail.

**Session Summary** in **[Copilot Share](https://marketplace.visualstudio.com/items?itemName=warlordy.copilot-share)** gives you a fast, reliable way to recover direction.

It turns long, noisy conversations into clear outcomes.

- Generate concise summaries of key topics, decisions, and next actions.
- Keep signal high while reducing chat noise.
