## Copilot Share: Find Past Prompts Fast and Avoid Rework

When using Copilot or other AI agents, it is easy to lose track of a prompt you already solved.

**Session Search** in **[Copilot Share](https://marketplace.visualstudio.com/items?itemName=warlordy.copilot-share)** helps you find it fast and avoid repeating work.

It helps you stop rewriting prompts you already solved.

- Search within the current session for precise context.
- Search across all sessions to find past prompts and high-value responses.
